THE RULES — SHORT VERSION
✅
Be Kind Always
Treat every kid on here the way you'd want to be treated. Encourage others, celebrate their work, and lift people up.
🎮
Share What You Make
Post your games, art, videos, card designs, science facts, and ideas. This is YOUR platform to show the world what kids can do.
🆘
Report Bad Stuff
If you see something that feels wrong, hit the Report button. Our safety team reviews every report within 24 hours.
🚫
No Bullying, Ever
Mean comments, insults, harassment, or anything designed to make someone feel bad is an instant ban. Zero tolerance.
🔒
No Personal Info
Never share your real full name, school, address, or phone number in posts or comments. Your safety comes first.
🤝
No Adult Strangers
Adults are not allowed to message kids on Kidstok. If any adult contacts you here, report it immediately and tell a parent.
FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
Can adults see my profile? ▼
Your profile is only visible to other verified kids on Kidstok. Adults cannot create accounts, and our age verification system blocks anyone 18 and over.
What happens when I report someone? ▼
A real human on our safety team reviews every report within 24 hours. If the report is valid, the account gets a warning or an instant ban depending on how serious it is. You'll get a notification when it's resolved.
Is Kidstok free forever? ▼
Yes! Kidstok is 100% free and will always be free. We don't run ads and don't sell your data. We're building this for kids, not for money.
What if I see something that scares me? ▼
First, don't panic. Hit the Report button and describe what you saw. Then tell a trusted adult in your life — a parent, teacher, or guardian. You can also use the emergency report button below which gets reviewed in under 1 hour.
Are kids with disabilities welcome here? ▼
ABSOLUTELY. Kidstok was built with inclusion as a core value. Kids with autism, ADHD, Tourette syndrome, physical disabilities, and every other condition are not just welcome — they're celebrated here. Any bullying related to disability is an immediate permanent ban.

🚨 See Something Wrong?

If someone is being mean, scary, or inappropriate — report it immediately. Every report goes straight to a real human on our safety team. You're not alone.
